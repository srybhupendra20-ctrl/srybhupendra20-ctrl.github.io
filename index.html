Aśvagandhā
Withania somnifera
Key Phytochemicals
Withanolides (Withaferin A, Withanolide D), Somniferine
Pharmacological Mechanism
Modulates HPA axis expression, reduces systemic serum cortisol levels, and acts as a competitive GABA-A receptor agonist to mitigate neurological stress induction.
Ref: Caraka Saṃhitā, Cikitsāsthāna
Haridrā
Curcuma longa
Key Phytochemicals
Curcuminoids (Curcumin, Demethoxycurcumin, Bisdemethoxycurcumin)
Pharmacological Mechanism
Downregulates NF-κB transcriptive signaling, suppressing synthesis of inflammatory cytokines (TNF-α, IL-1β) and inhibiting COX-2 enzyme profiles.
Ref: Suśruta Saṃhitā, Sūtrasthāna
Tulasī
Ocimum sanctum
Key Phytochemicals
Eugenol, Ursolic Acid, Rosmarinic Acid, Carvacrol
Pharmacological Mechanism
Enhances immunomodulatory parameters by maximizing Natural Killer (NK) cell activity and scavenging intracellular Reactive Oxygen Species (ROS).
Ref: Dhanvantari Nighaṇṭu
Brāhmī
Bacopa monnieri
Key Phytochemicals
Bacosides (A & B), Hersaponin, Monnierin
Pharmacological Mechanism
Upregulates protein kinase transcription in the hippocampus, facilitating synaptic repair and reinforcing endogenous antioxidant enzymes like Superoxide Dismutase (SOD).
Ref: Caraka Saṃhitā, Sūtrasthāna
Nimba
Azadirachta indica
Key Phytochemicals
Azadirachtin, Nimbin, Nimbidin, Gedunin
Pharmacological Mechanism
Disrupts bacterial peptidoglycan wall architectures, prevents biofilm clustering, and exerts antipyretic activity by mitigating central prostaglandin synthesis.
Ref: Suśruta Saṃhitā, Sūtrasthāna
Guḍūcī
Tinospora cordifolia
Key Phytochemicals
Tinosporaside, Cordifolioside A, Berberine
Pharmacological Mechanism
Induces differentiation within macrophage populations, amplifies cellular IgG antibody populations, and restricts lipid peroxidation across hepatocyte membranes.
Ref: Bhāvaprakāśa Nighaṇṭu
Śatāvarī
Asparagus racemosus
Key Phytochemicals
Shatavarins (I-IV), Sarsasapogenin
Pharmacological Mechanism
Enhances total white blood cell output, stimulates phagocytic capacity in peritoneal macrophages, and exhibits systematic mucosal lining protection.
Ref: Aṣṭāṅga Hṛdayam, Cikitsāsthāna
Āmalakī
Phyllanthus emblica
Key Phytochemicals
Emblicanin A & B, Ascorbic Acid
Pharmacological Mechanism
Acts as a robust antioxidant pathway donor, neutralizing mitochondrial free radical accumulations and downregulating age-related DNA damage.
Ref: Caraka Saṃhitā, Cikitsāsthāna

Convolvulus pluricaulis
Key Phytochemicals
Convolvine, Phyllabine, Pluricaulin
Pharmacological Mechanism
Reduces expression profiles of acetylcholinesterase (AChE) enzymes, increasing acetylcholine accessibility to lower amygdala anxiety scores.
